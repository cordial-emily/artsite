Egregore

Sloppy Sonnet #3

By Emily Schleiner December 2023

“...is an occult concept representing a certain non-physical entity that arises from the collective thoughts of a distinct group of people.”
Wikipedia

If an artificial intelligence
is made up from on and off switches,
and a human mind comes from cell correspondence,
then what presence emerges and flinches

...when groups of people have a distinct thought
with enough complexity to grow.
Is the presence peaceful or overwrought,
An initiator of healing or shadow?

My meditation groups’ egregore
gentles my thoughts and grants me wide spaces,
while my moon group’s tremor
gives my underdog aspects deep embraces.

What egregores will I co-create
in future groups indeterminate?
