Artistic Odyssey

Sloppy Sonnet #1

By Emily Schleiner June 2023

I absorbed critiques,
read Art Forum, Tufte, Goldberg, and Foucault.
Context was my vehicle.
Whimsy and truth would flow.

After Mcluhen, Manovich, and Turkle,
I increased my academic means,
I was no longer paradigm senseless.
Made objects intended for museums.

Drafting and crafting, I applied
to grants, conferences, and teaching posts.
Only debt did I find.
And technology came into focus.

Logic became my friend. And art unfurled
a tie to my heart, which re-connects me to the world.
