Social Media

Sloppy Sonnet #4

By Emily Schleiner March 2024

“If I feel especially great posting something, that’s when I know I’m going to feel terrible later.”

Like a Las Vegas light show with
clever memes, familiar faces, stories,
moments of delicious discovery,
and topics that trigger.

I hop in the shallow end, confident,
typing responses, sleeping soundly,
checking only once per day.
Later to find myself at the center

of a town square where words echo,
traveling through friends of friends,
vibrating into the future, chaotic,
drawing energy from me into the

commercial, copyrighted material of a
being. A social media company.
