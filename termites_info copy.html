Termites

Size: 12” x 12” x 30”
Media: mixed media, sculpture
Materials: paper mache, mesh wire, soil, acrylic paints, glass, iPod

12-11-12 Davis, California. Termites is a sculpture that contains video of a busy termite colony carrying around items that would be used in a hacker space. On their backs they carry computers, chairs, an oscilloscope, transistors, and more. This piece was shown in ORGANISM, An Art Show Fusing Art, Science and Technology in December 2012 at the Old Nelson Gallery on UC Davis Campus. Later shown in the 17th International Conference on Information Visualization SOAS online gallery, University of London.
