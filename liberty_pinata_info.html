Liberty Piñata

Size: 10’ x 10’ x 20’ Media: performance, sculpture, mixed media, acrylic paints, Materials: paper mache, acrylic paint, rope, bat

6-27-08 Governor’s Island, New York. This piñata of the statue of liberty is stuffed with credit cards and candy. Pennies are on a portion of the outside. This performance happened on Governor's Island in the Figment Art Festival.Figment Art Festival.
