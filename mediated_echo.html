Mediated Echo

Size: 10' x 10' x 10'
Media: installation, mixed media, video
Materials: Webcam, computer, mixed media, video projector

9-2-16 This project makes a life-size kaleidoscope from enlarged plants and live footage so that people can interact with the footage in a triangular echo chamber, only viewable by others who look into the sculpture. Inspired by the parable "In heaven we feed each other", the piece uses a live video feed, and footage of plants and other small things. This installation was originally shown at A1labarts at the 2016 Signification Show.
